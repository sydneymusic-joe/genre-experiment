Welcome!

SydneyMusic.net is a not-for-profit, community-centric gig guide.

Blind Date With A Band is small hack project built by Joe Hardy while doing a quick data-gathering experiment with around 100 local Sydney bands on our Instagram account last week.

The data is updated daily and you can use this service as a different kind of gig discovery tool. New recommendations will appear in the mix as they're added to the gig guide.

Any band featured on this site is playing a show in the next 4 weeks and defined the genre themselves.

If you would like to add your band to the genre tagging experiment, then please send us an Instagram DM from your official band account to authenticate your submission. We can't guarantee a turnaround time, but we'll do our best.

We plan to incorporate this data into our gig guide but that will take a little longer and requires resources we don't currently have.
